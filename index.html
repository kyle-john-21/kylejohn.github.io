About Me

Hi, I am Jun ZENG, a forth year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the National University of Singapore, working with Zhenkai Liang. I received my B.E. degree in Computer Science at the Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2018.

My research interests lie in the interactions of system security and recommendation systems. Specifically, I am working on designing recommendation engines for audit log analysis and program analysis.

Apart from Computer Science, I enjoy playing DOTA2 (Ranking: Divine), traveling and watching movies.
